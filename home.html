The Artist Behind the Cone

Fatima Rabiu

Welcome to Baby Henna! I am Fatima Rabiu, a passionate henna artist specialized in turning traditional body art into breathtaking statements. From delicate, minimalist lines to heavily detailed bridal packages, each pattern is hand-drawn to accentuate your style for weddings, Eid celebrations, or special moments.

We prioritize organic, skin-safe henna pastes to produce rich, beautiful stains that stand out. Explore my artwork gallery above, choose a concept that fits you perfectly, and drop your details to secure a session!
